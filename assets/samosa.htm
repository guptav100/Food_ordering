    7 Most Popular Indian Food in the World in 2019

    September 20, 2019 In Indian Foods

    Indian cuisines are known all over the world for their mouth-watering taste and mind-blowing aroma. Every state has its own dishes that represents its culture and simply taste heavenly. In this blog, we’ve provided a list of 7 most popular Indian foods in the world. We would recommend you to visit a trusted Indian restaurant in Colorado Springs and savor the taste of each of them.

     

    • Samosa

    Samosas are undeniably the best Indian starters. These are fried patty stuffed with flavored mashed potatoes and served hot along with chutney. The best part about this dish is that it is extremely easy to prepare. A dough of flour is formed into cones and filled with fillings of potatoes and seasonal vegetables. Then, they are then deep-fried.

     

    • Chicken Tikka Masala

    Chicken Tikka Masala is one of the most popular non-veg cuisines not only in India but also in other parts of the globe. Cooked in spicy gravy and heavily laced with butter, this delicious charcoal burnt, clay oven cooked chicken has never failed to excite the taste buds of foodies. The fiery red, crispy-on-the-outside, succulent-on-the-inside dish has taken the world by storm ever since it was first served during the times of the Mughals.

     

    • Butter Chicken

    Unarguably, this is one of the most popular Indian dishes not just in the sub-continent, but also across the globe. This is the reason why most Indian restaurants in Colorado Springs offer this dish. Traditionally cooked in tandoor, butter chicken is a dish that every non-vegetarian is fond of! These are soft boneless chicken pieces cooked in butter, tomato, onion & garam masala. Visit a Colorado Springs fine dining restaurant today to taste authentic Butter Chicken.

     

    • Biryani

    Biryani is one of the most popular Indian cuisines that is served in all parts of the world. Experts say, this is a classic concoction of rice and chicken and has more variants than any other dish in the world. The best part about this dish is that preparing it doesn’t involve much hassle. Chicken or mutton is marinated in curd along with fragrant spices. It is then mixed with rice and slow-cooked for hours. Going forward, the rice is mixed with a pre-cooked gravy to make it moist and flavorsome.

     

    • Palak Paneer

    Though this dish originated in the Indian state of Punjab, it gained popularity all over India over the years. Now, it’s one of the most sought-after vegan dishes globally. If you’re a veggie and want your main course to be healthy and delicious, then Palak Paneer is definitely the best option for you! Made with Spinach and Paneer (cottage cheese), it is one of the most popular vegetarian dishes in India. Eat it along with naans or chapatis and excite your taste buds.

     

    • Dal Makhni

    This is yet another Punjabi Dish that gained immense popularity over the years. We think it’s a perfect vegetarian variant for butter chicken. A mix of lentils is slow-cooked with tomato puree and butter and served hot. Eat it along with Roti or Naan and relish the mouth-watering taste of Dal Makhni.

     

    • Malai Kofta

    Malai Kofta is the vegetarian version of meatballs. This is made either with gourd or unripe bananas. The taste may vary from mild to very spicy. The way of preparing this dish is also simple. It’s cooked in a gravy of tomato, cream, and spices. Then, the balls are soaked in the flavors and served hot with bread or rice.
